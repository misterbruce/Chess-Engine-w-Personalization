# Chess-Engine-w-Personalization
This project is dedicated to my younger cousin who I hope uses this to improve and gain the skill to one day beat me in chess. 

Little about the project:
  When I am at family functions and gatherings my uncle will sometimes bring up chess to me and how he would be grateful if I tought his son to play as well as I do. Now I am all for learning chess and playing it but I would realistically have no time to teach my young cousin chess. My solution? Well to implement computer science into an ultra-focus chess training MACHINE!

  When I first picked up this project I approached it with a curiousity to if I could do this without external libraries or tutorial. Needless to say I appreciate them now. However, I have reached a much greater understanding of the coding language Python in exchange for this pleasant headache. 

  Though as this project grows I will extend my attention to tutorials and to libraries to help make this thing possible. The end goal is for this device to be fun and also informative on how to play good chess (but without the heavy theory involved).

Key Points:
- A device soley for playing and improving at chess.
- The device glows red or green and plays corresponding pleasant or unpleasant sounds depending on strength of user's move.
- Only PvC.
- Games are to be stored for tactic and puzzle generation as well as potential bot training to target and for weakness correction of user.
